Escrow Tracker

Escrow Tracker is a custom app built on the Salesforce platform that tracks dates, contract documents and all parties involved with my client's third party escrow process. It comes with a robust workflow that ensures important tasks are not missed and a color coded dashboard for quick viewing of important milestones related to the escrow. If a deadline is approaching and the assigned user does not acknowledge, emails are sent to other team members to prevent any lack of transparency.

Project Management Office

Project Management Office (PMO) is an application built with SharePoint 2013 OOTB features combined with jQuery for visual enhancements and full AD integration. It serves primarily as an intake portal for end users to submit opportunities and upgrades for official approval. Custom end user and administrator views allow for efficient display of data as well as a public status page for all employees. With a powerful workflow under the hood, PMO can handle any situation.
